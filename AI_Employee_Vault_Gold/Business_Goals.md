# Business Goals for AI Employee

## Primary Objectives
- Increase monthly recurring revenue by 15%
- Improve customer response time to under 2 hours
- Automate 80% of routine business operations
- Generate 10 qualified leads per month through digital channels
- Reduce operational costs by 10% through efficiency improvements

## Monthly Targets
- Revenue: $25,000
- New customers: 5
- Customer retention rate: 95%
- Social media engagement: 5% increase
- Invoice collection rate: 98%

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Average response time to customer inquiries
- Number of invoices processed per week
- Social media follower growth rate
- Customer satisfaction score
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- Cost per acquisition (CAC)

## Strategic Initiatives
- Expand service offerings to include consulting
- Develop partnerships with complementary businesses
- Implement referral program
- Create educational content to establish thought leadership
- Optimize pricing strategy based on market research

## Operational Metrics
- Tasks completed per day: 20
- Approval requests processed per day: 10
- Social posts published per week: 7
- Emails responded to per day: 15
- New leads generated per week: 3

## Weekly Review Points
- Revenue tracking (MTD and weekly)
- Task completion rates
- Customer satisfaction scores
- Social media performance metrics
- Operational efficiency measures

## Proactive Actions
- Identify and cancel unused subscriptions
- Follow up on overdue invoices
- Monitor competitor activities
- Suggest process improvements
- Flag potential business opportunities
